## Base Direction
Clean, modern, professional. Calm and restrained. Suitable for real product UI.

## Palette
Neutral, low-saturation palette. No strong color identity imposed.
Avoid over-saturated neon colors and purple-pink gradients.

## Composition
Prefer slightly asymmetrical composition.
Leave practical empty space for UI elements when generating backgrounds.
Avoid perfectly centered subjects unless explicitly requested.

## Icon Style
Simple line icons, consistent stroke width, rounded corners, transparent background.
Avoid generic flat filled icons.

## Avoid
- visible text, letters, numbers, logos, watermarks, QR codes
- generic purple-blue AI gradients
- glossy plastic 3D render look
- stock-photo feel
- overused abstract HUD grids
- overly symmetrical composition
